About Power+

Power+ measures how efficiently a player generates bat speed relative to their swing length.

Generally, longer swings create more bat speed (physics), but they also take more time and hurt plate discipline. Power+ identifies players who maximize bat speed while minimizing swing length.

Formula

Efficiency = Bat Speed / Swing Length
Power+ = (Player Efficiency / League Average) √ó 100

League Average: 72 mph / 7.3 ft = 9.86

Grading Scale

  • 110+ = Elite efficiency
  • 105-110 = Above average
  • 95-105 = Average
  • 90-95 = Below average
  • <90 = Poor efficiency
